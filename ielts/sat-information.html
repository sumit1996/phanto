IELTS Test Information

The IELTS is a standardized test required by many colleges and universities as part of their admission process. The score a student gets on the IELTS is often used as a predictor of how a student is likely to perform in English language. An IELTS score is not supposed to be used as the sole admissions criterion admissions committees should also consider under graduate grades, recommendations, essays and other relevant information in offering places on their graduate courses.

In its present form it consists of two different examinations:

  • The IELTS General Test
  • The IELTS Academic Test
Listening and Speaking are the same for both tests, but the subject matter of the Reading and Writing components differs depending on which test you take.
